Macaulay2

Try out Macaulay2 right here on this interactive website. Macaulay2 is a software system devoted to supporting research in algebraic geometry and commutative algebra, whose creation has been funded by the National Science Foundation since 1992. You can also download and install Macaulay2 on your machine. Choose a lesson, and have fun!
